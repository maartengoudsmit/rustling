There's an art to showing off a typeface. A proper font showcase page sells, inspires and delights the reader. Here are some best-in-class web pages that do just that.

### Fraunces by Undercase Type

The font I use as the header for this very site. Both stately and playful at the same time, it occupies a tidy mid-point between Cooper Black and Bookman Swash. {{ "[https://fraunces.undercase.xyz/](https://fraunces.undercase.xyz/)" | hostname }}![](/media/Fraunces.png)

### Playwrite by Type Together

An effort by Type Together to document and preserve traditional handwriting styles taught in primary schools around the world. The project has resulted in a truly magnificent collection of typefaces. Moreover, [its website](https://primarium.info/) showcases the many documents used in the creation of the final artifacts.
